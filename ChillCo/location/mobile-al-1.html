Chiller Company Serving Businesses in the Mobile, AL, Area

ChillCo is a leading chilling company offering comprehensive cooling solutions in the Mobile, Alabama, area. As one of the largest independent companies in the Gulf South specializing in commercial chiller plants and industrial process refrigeration, we are dedicated to helping our clients prevent and resolve both routine and catastrophic cooling system issues. With a multi-faceted and comprehensive approach to business, we deliver tailored solutions to meet the unique needs of each customer.

‍

Industrial Chiller Rental Services

‍

When unexpected equipment failures occur or planned shutdowns are necessary, ChillCo offers comprehensive chiller rental solutions to keep your operations running smoothly. Our extensive inventory of portable rental chillers and boilers is available on flexible terms to accommodate your specific needs. Whether you require air-cooled or water-cooled units, our rentals are designed to provide reliable cooling for applications such as inlet air cooling and production bottlenecks. With rapid delivery and setup, our trailer-mounted chiller rentals in Mobile, minimize downtime and maximize productivity for your business.

‍

Field Service

‍

Our highly skilled field service team is dedicated to ensuring the smooth and efficient operation of your industrial refrigeration and commercial HVAC systems . With expertise in maintaining chillers used in various applications, including industrial processes and medical imaging, we provide comprehensive technical services to prevent minor issues from escalating into costly breakdowns. From planned maintenance to compressor repair and annual inspections, ChillCo's proactive maintenance approach helps you avoid downtime and extend the lifespan of your equipment, specializing in absorption chiller services in Mobile, AL.

‍

Compressor Reconditioning

‍

Keep your industrial refrigeration or air conditioning system running smoothly with ChillCo's compressor reconditioning services. Our skilled technicians are experts in reconditioning screw and centrifugal compressors. By bringing compressors from top manufacturers back to like-new condition, we offer affordable protection against major breakdowns and ensure peak system performance. Our meticulous process involves disassembly, cleaning, inspection, and reassembly, delivering reliable performance for years to come. Trust ChillCo for precision and quality in compressor maintenance.

Training

ChillCo's extensive training programs can boost your facility's workforce capabilities. Tailored to elevate technician confidence, skill levels, and productivity, our courses encompass a broad spectrum of maintenance, operations, and engineering topics. Prioritizing safety, efficiency, and equipment reliability, our training delivers both immediate and enduring advantages to your organization. Whether your team comprises maintenance technicians, operators, or engineers, ChillCo has the expertise and resources to fulfill your requirements. Our customizable training initiatives are adept at addressing unique challenges and objectives, guaranteeing optimal impact for your team's development.

‍

Refrigerant Recycling

‍

ChillCo offers a diverse range of refrigerant recycling solutions to meet your environmental and regulatory needs. Our state-of-the-art recycling system, "The Ox," enables us to recycle halocarbon refrigerants without disrupting your production processes. Our efficient vapor recovery process minimizes downtime, allowing for faster access to your refrigeration system for repairs, and ensuring smooth operations with minimal disruptions. With a focus on safety and compliance, we adhere to EPA and OSHA guidelines, ensuring proper documentation of all refrigeration system processes.
