Field Service

ChillCo’s field service team keeps industrial refrigeration and large tonnage commercial HVAC running reliably, efficiently and economically. ChillCo has particular expertise in maintaining chillers utilized in industrial process, medical imaging and comfort cooling applications.

You can rely on ChillCo to prevent small issues from developing into costly breakdowns! Our comprehensive technical service assures trouble-free cooling system operation.
