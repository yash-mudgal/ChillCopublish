Why Rent an Air-Cooled Chiller Unit?

Air-cooled chiller rentals offer many benefits, so they are ideal for various industrial and commercial applications. Understanding these benefits can help you make an informed decision about your cooling needs.

Cost-Effective Cooling Rental Solutions

One of the primary air-cooled chiller benefits is cost efficiency. Our air-cooled chiller rentals eliminate the need for expensive installation and maintenance associated with permanent systems. You can enjoy the cooling capacity you need without worrying about long-term commitments or high maintenance costs.

Easy Installation and Maintenance

Unlike water-cooled chillers, which require a complex network of cooling towers and water pumps, air-cooled chillers use ambient air to dissipate heat. This eliminates the need for additional infrastructure, reducing installation time and costs. Additionally, air-cooled chillers have fewer components, making them easier to maintain and service.

Versatility and Flexibility

Air-cooled chillers are highly versatile and can be used in a wide range of applications. They are suitable for indoor and outdoor installations and can be deployed in various settings. Their flexibility makes them an excellent choice for situations where space is limited or where water resources are scarce.

Energy Efficiency

Modern air-cooled chillers maximize energy efficiency. They incorporate advanced technologies such as variable speed drives, high-efficiency compressors, and microchannel heat exchangers, which enhance performance and reduce energy consumption. By choosing an air-cooled chiller rental from ChillCo, you can benefit from these energy-saving features and potentially lower operational costs.

Comprehensive Field Services for Air-Cooled Chiller Rentals

Our field services ensure your equipment operates smoothly. Our technicians can handle all aspects of field service, from installation and setup to routine maintenance and emergency repairs. Our field services include:

  • Planned maintenance – Regular maintenance is essential for your equipment’s efficiency. We offer planned maintenance services to keep your air handlers in top condition.
  • Compressor repair/service/rebuilds – Our team is skilled in repairing and servicing compressors.
  • Annual inspections – We conduct thorough annual inspections to identify any potential issues before they become major problems.
  • Planned outages – We provide support during planned outages, ensuring that your cooling needs are met without interruption.
  • Maintenance service agreements – Our service agreements offer peace of mind, knowing that your equipment is covered for regular maintenance and repairs.
  • Vibration analysis – We use advanced techniques to analyze and mitigate vibration issues that can affect your equipment’s lifespan.
  • Multi-year stop inspections – For long-term projects, we offer multi-year stop inspections to ensure continuous operation.
  • Oil, refrigerant, and lithium bromide analysis – We provide detailed analysis of essential fluids to maintain optimal performance and prevent issues.
  • Eddy current tube analysis – This non-destructive testing method allows us to assess the condition of your equipment and prevent failures.
  • Retube of evaporator and condenser – Our experts can replace tubes in evaporators and condensers to extend the lifespan of your units.
  • Process gas recovery and recycling – We offer environmentally responsible solutions for gas recovery and recycling.
  • Refrigerant clean-up kits – Our refrigerant clean-up kits ensure your system remains clean.
  • High-efficiency purge units – We provide high-efficiency purge units to improve your system’s longevity.
  • PLC system upgrades – We offer upgrades to your PLC systems to enhance control.
